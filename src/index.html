Hi there & welcome to my site!

My name is Hans-Peter Dietz and I like to think of myself as a fullstack anything coder meaning I neither resort to any one particular programming language nor field when coding. I do, however, prefer unixy environments.

After finishing my master's degree in Media Informatics at Ludwig-Maximilians-University, Munich, I started out doing commisional web and mobile development as well as consulting with J&H.

At the moment I am employed as a Software Engineer at CHECK24 working as fullstack JavaScript developer with some DevOps on the side. I also help out with native mobile development in times of need.

In my spare time I play the bass guitar in the post-/progressive rock band Watersun and take my motorcycle for a ride. Furthermore, apart from coding my interests expand into electronics and physics.
